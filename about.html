Gameify and Incentivise

As a due date approaches the color of the chore changes (red, yellow and green traffic lights). Game-ify it, each chore is worth 10 points some are worth more, the most points at the end of the week checked off. Checking off chores quickly gives you more points. ChoreScore - each chore has the same "baseline" points value, but completing chores quickly allows "Child" to earn "Bonus Points", and CleanStreaks - a metric to track how consistently each "Child" completes their assigned chores.
